The site fetches posts from curated RSS sources and filters them for West Bengal-only content
using a strict keyword list (districts/cities + 'West Bengal', 'Kolkata', etc.).

Current sources:
- Indian Express (Kolkata city): https://indianexpress.com/section/cities/kolkata/feed/
- Indian Express (Jobs): https://indianexpress.com/section/jobs/feed/
- Hindustan Times (RSS hub): https://www.hindustantimes.com/rss
- ABP Ananda (Bengali RSS hub): https://bengali.abplive.com/rss
- Times of India (RSS hub): https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/rss.cms
- Press Information Bureau (All Releases): https://www.pib.gov.in/ViewRss.aspx
- Press Information Bureau (Regional East): https://pib.gov.in/RssMain.aspx?ModId=6&Lang=1&Regid=3
- KGP Chronicle (IIT Kharagpur): https://kgpchronicle.iitkgp.ac.in/feed/

You can add/remove feeds in data/feeds.json. The frontend will auto-pick it up on next deploy.
