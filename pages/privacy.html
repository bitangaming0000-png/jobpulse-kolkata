Introduction

JobPulse Kolkata (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a news and information website focused on job and exam updates relevant to West Bengal. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping the experience simple, transparent, and secure.

Information we collect

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. AdSense may use cookies to show personalized or non-personalized ads. You can manage ad personalization in your Google account. By using our site, you consent to the use of such cookies by Google in accordance with their policy.

Links to other websites

Posts often link to external sites (official notices, portals, or trusted job boards). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to respond and maintain records. We do not sell personal data.

Your rights

You can request deletion of messages you submitted via the contact form, or ask questions about our data practices by contacting us through the Contact page.

Changes
