About JobPulse Kolkata

Last updated:

JobPulse Kolkata is an independent, fast and distraction-free site focused on West Bengal jobs, exams, and admit card/result updates. We aggregate official announcements and trusted job portals via RSS, then rewrite and reformat them so they’re easier to scan on mobile. Our goal is simple: help candidates in Kolkata and across WB discover relevant vacancies quickly and apply at the official source.

What we publish

  • Government jobs (WBPSC, WB Police, Health, Education, Panchayat, etc.)
  • Bank & Rail updates relevant to candidates in West Bengal
  • Private and IT openings located in WB or remote roles open to WB residents
  • Exam dates, admit cards, results, and official notices

How we curate content

We monitor multiple trusted sources using RSS feeds and official websites. Each item is rewritten to avoid duplication and to add value—clear headings, important dates, eligibility and application steps. We always link back to the official website so candidates can verify details and apply safely.

Quality & transparency

  • No paywall: the website is free to access.
  • Source links: every post includes the source URL where possible.
  • Corrections: if something looks wrong, contact us and we’ll fix it.

Revenue & ads

We use Google AdSense for basic display ads. Ads help us keep the site free and maintain hosting. We don’t sell personal data; see our Privacy Policy.

Our promise

We keep the UI clean, fast and accessible. Features like dark mode, live ticker, auto-scrolling lists, “Save Job”, weather/clock widgets and an exam calendar are designed to make browsing efficient without getting in your way.
