Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about using JobPulse Kolkata — jobs, alerts, sources, and more.

Is JobPulse Kolkata free to use?
Yes. Browsing jobs, news and alerts here is completely free.
How do I apply to a job I see here?
Open the job card → click Original Source or Apply. You’ll be redirected to the official site/employer.
Do you guarantee placements or hiring?
No. We only share information. Always verify details and deadlines on the official source.
How can I get alerts for new jobs?
Check Notifications and Latest Announcements on Home. You can also follow our social channels or communities if shared.
Are work-from-home jobs available?
Yes. Use the WFH category or the checkbox filter on the Jobs page.
Do you cover all districts in West Bengal?
We try to cover Kolkata, Howrah, Siliguri, Asansol, Durgapur, Haldia, Kharagpur, Burdwan, Jalpaiguri, Malda, Bankura, Purulia and more via the By Location sections.
Can I submit a job to list on the site?
Yes — send details through the Contact page. We review and publish suitable listings.
How do you make money?
Primarily through ads (e.g., Google AdSense). See our Privacy Policy for data and cookie use.
